About Us

Loyalty car wash is a young, fresh and mobile car valeting business, with a very individual business model. We strive to give something back to the world and make a difference in peoples lives. We have been able to achieve this by annually donating a percentage of our proceeds, to create individual water wells in Africa to provide fresh drinking water to the people African.

Our Mission is to provide premium service to our customers and a great work environment for our employees, while providing the community with a respectable and professional mobile car wash service.
